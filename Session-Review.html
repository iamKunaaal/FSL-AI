1. The Hook: Digital Shadow (15 mins)

Begin by asking students to search for a product they recently looked at. Discuss how quickly relevant ads appear. Use the "Digital Shadow" metaphor to explain data accumulation.

Discussion Prompt:

"If you could see all the data a brand knows about you right now, what would surprise you most? Your location? Your mood? Your budget?"

2. Deconstruction Lab (40 mins)

  1. Divide students into groups of three.
  2. Assign each group a "Viral Brand" case study.
  3. Task: Identify the specific emotional trigger used in the ad (Fear of Missing Out, Social Proof, or Scarcity).
  4. Map the user journey from "Ad View" to "Data Point Creation".

3. Reflection & Synthesis (25 mins)

Groups present their findings. Introduce the "Ethical Filter" checklist for students to use in upcoming sessions.
